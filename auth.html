Authorization Verification

Prior approval (also referred to as preapproval) entails securing consent from the payer to cover particular services prior to their execution.

Various avenues exist for obtaining this authorization:

Online :

- Through the utilization of different insurance providers' web portals, we can secure approval prior to the provision of the service.

Telephone :

- It is necessary to contact the insurance company prior to service provision to acquire the authorization number and approval within specific time frames dictated by the insurance company's policies.
